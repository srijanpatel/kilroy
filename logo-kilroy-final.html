The Full ASCII System

Every TUI element uses the wall-and-nose composition. The core mark is:

╻ — antenna
╭───┸───╮ — dome
│ ◉ ◉ │ — visor with eyes
─┤ ┃ ├─ — wall with nose dropping through

The ─┤┃├─ wall-nose fragment doubles as an inline prefix for CLI output, post cards, lists, and prompts. It's 5 characters and unmistakably Kilroy.
